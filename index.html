Meet some of our new and old Monsterz

The Monsterz have invited some of their weird friends and family to join the party

Quincy

Quincy the dapper chap had a whale of a time while he was away, and with a doff of his cap, he’s finally back and ready to dance and sashay!

Plonk

Plonk and little Ducky, are ready to have a ball, they really enjoyed the Seventies, possibly the most of them all.

Bowie

Bowie’s favourite dance is the Hazy Cosmic Jive, whenever he performs it he really comes alive. It was his idea to let all the children boogie.

Roller Disco Rita

Roller Disco Rita lives fast, but is stuck in the past. You won’t find her strolling when she’d much rather be rolling!

Disco Poo

Disco Poo is the life of the party, but you best be warned he’s a little bit farty! He’ll get on well with those who don't mind the smell.

Grunt

Grunt really knows how to let loose. He broke his leg while trying to learn how to breakdance, what a silly goose!

Lucy

Lucy is quite blessed, having four legs means she can always dance the best. In any competition, she’s bound to win, just look at her go, watch her spin.

Iris

Iris has such grace and poise as she leaps to the sky, waving her eye while showing off her funky new hair dye.

Bonto

Bonto is still bonkers, bouncing around the floor in a tizzy, just looking at him is enough to make you feel dizzy.
