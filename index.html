Brain Tumour Ireland

In 2019 one of our team was diagnosed with a Brain Tumour. The size of a lemon they said. Funny that... do you mean a big lemon or a little tiny one? Like us they come in all shapes and sizes. Either way it was a bit of a shock. But the first thing to come out of their mouth was "Let's make lemonade".

Life was a little bitter for the next few years but with the addition of bucket loads of sugar from wizard doctors, magical friends, family and Brain Tumour Ireland, life is getting back to normal.

Marino Monsterz was created to lift the spirits of all the families in the Marino community during a hard time. But it lifted our spirits making it. And any money raised will lift the spirits of children in Ireland who are affected by Brain Tumours. Be it themselves, or a member of their family.

Brain Tumour Ireland is a non-profit organization. As is the Marino Monsterz project. We're just giving fun, creativity and love to those who need it.

Meet some of our new and old Monsterz

The Monsterz have invited some of their weird friends and family to join the party

Quincy

Quincey the dapper chap had a whale of a time while he was away, and with a doff of his cap, he’s finally back and ready to dance and sashay!

Plonk

Plonk and little Ducky, are ready to have a ball, they really enjoyed the Seventies, possibly the most of them all.

Bowie

Bowie’s favourite dance is the Hazy Cosmic Jive, whenever he performs it he really comes alive. It was his idea to let all the children boogie.

Roller Disco Rita

Roller Disco Rita lives fast, but she’s stuck in the past. You won’t find her strolling when she’d much rather be rolling!

Disco Poo

Disco Poo is the life of the party, but you best be warned he’s a little bit farty! He’ll get on well with anyone who can stand the smell.

Grunt

Grunt really knows how to let loose. He broke his leg while trying to learn how to breakdance, what a silly goose!

Lucy

Lucy is quite blessed, having four legs means she can always dance the best. In any competition, she’s bound to win, just look at her go, watch her spin.

Iris

Iris has such grace and poise as she leaps to the sky, waving her eye while showing off her funky new hair dye.

Bonto

Bonto is still bonkers, bouncing around the floor in a tizzy, just looking at him is enough to make you feel dizzy.
